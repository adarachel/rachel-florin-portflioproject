NELSON MANDELA

Rebel, Warrior, Leader. And A Man Who Inspired A Nation And The World.

PRESIDENT MANDELA

Here's A Timeline Of His Life
(18 July 1918 to 5 December 2013):


  • 1918 : Born Rolihlahla Mandela at Mvezo in the Transkei
  • 1925 : Attends primary school near Qunu (receives the name ‘Nelson’ from a teacher)
  • 1930 : Father dies. Entrusted to Thembu Regent Jongintaba Dalindyebo at the age of 12.
  • 1934 : Undergoes initiation; Attends Clarkebury Boarding Institute in Engcobo
  • 1937 : Attends Healdtown, the Wesleyan College at Fort Beauforts
  • 1994 : Inaugurated as President of the Republic of South Africa
  • 1996 : Divorces Winnie Mandela
  • 2012 : Celebrates his 94th birthday with his family in Qunu, Transkei
  • December 5, 2013 : Passes away at home in Johannesburg

“Everyone can rise above their circumstances and achieve success if they are dedicated to and passionate about what they do.”
